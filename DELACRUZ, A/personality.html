My Personality

Friendly and outgoing: I’m the type of person who can make friends anywhere. I don’t like being left out, and I always find ways to connect with others.

Honest and direct: I say what I feel, especially when something bothers me. I believe in speaking up rather than keeping things inside.

Empathetic listener: When friends open up to me, I listen without judgment. I understand how important it is to feel heard.

Curious and a learner: I’m always exploring things I don’t know. I enjoy learning, especially in technology, music, and psychology.

Diskarte mindset: I don’t just work hard—I work smart. I always try to find creative and practical ways to solve problems.

"I am Aldrin, a friendly and honest person who values connection, listens with empathy, speaks with confidence, and always finds smart ways to solve problems—guided by curiosity, faith, and a heart that wants to grow and help others."
