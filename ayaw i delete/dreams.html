My Dreams

Personal Dreams

My biggest personal dream is to become financially successful so I can spoil my family with everything they want and deserve. I want to take them—along with my girlfriend on unforgettable trips to New Zealand and Japan. I dream of building a big house filled with love and laughter, and having a refrigerator that's always full of delicious food. All of this is not just for comfort, but to show how much I appreciate the people who stood by me through everything.

Career Dreams

In my career, I dream of becoming a skilled and respected IT professional. I want to build systems that are not only useful, but meaningful—something that makes life better for others. Whether it's developing apps, managing networks, or helping solve tech problems, I aim to keep learning and growing. One day, I also hope to start my own tech-related business that helps my community and gives back in a real way.
