Challenges in IT

Starting From Zero

I entered IT without any background in programming or hardware. It felt overwhelming at first, like learning a new language completely from scratch.

Facing Technical Errors

Sometimes, my code just wouldn't work and I didn’t know why. Debugging was frustrating—but it also taught me patience and persistence.

Internet Limitations

Living in a suburban area, I faced challenges with unstable or slow internet, which made it hard to join online classes or research technical topics.

Fear of Not Being Good Enough

Seeing others more advanced than me made me feel insecure at times. But instead of comparing, I now use that as motivation to grow at my own pace.
