Favorite Bible Verse

"When the time is right, I, the Lord, will make it happen."
— Isaiah 60:22
This verse reminds me that everything in life happens for a reason and in God's perfect timing. I may not understand the delays or the hardships I go through, but I trust that when the time is right, God will open doors I never expected. It gives me peace and encourages me to keep going, knowing that He’s in control and that His plans are better than mine.
