Why I Chose IT

Growing up, I really didn’t have any idea about the tech world because I lived in a suburban area. Back then, we didn’t even know much about gadgets. But things and perspectives change, over time, I realized the value of technology in this generation.

I believe IT is more than just coding, it’s about creating solutions that help people, connect the world, and make life easier.

I chose IT because I want to build meaningful systems, learn continuously, and become part of the IT world soon.
