WHERE I AM FROM

I am from Asuncion Davao del Norte, a peaceful and humble place. It’s where I grew up and made many memories that shaped who I am.

WHERE I WAS BORN

I was born on August 04, 2005 in Tulunan North Cotabato, in our small payag (yes, i was born inside of our house) that my family always remembers. It was a simple beginning but full of love.

MY RELIGION

The name of our church is Buclad General Baptist Church, located at Prk 1. Buclad, Asuncion, Dvao del Norte. It is the place that helped me grow spiritually and develop my talents.Through this church, I was taught what is right—especially about salvation. I am proud to say that God is using me through this church to be a blessing to everyone around me. And all the Glory and Honor, only belong to God.
