My Habits

Here are some of the habits that are part of my daily life — the good, the bad, and everything in between.

✨ Good Habits

  • Waking up early for school
  • Praying before sleeping
  • Greeting people with a smile
  • Organizing my things when needed
  • Listening to others patiently

⚠️ Habits to Improve

  • Sleeping late too often
  • Procrastinating when I feel lazy
  • Using my phone too much
  • Being too slow in the morning
  • Not asking for help even when I need it
  • Sometimes saying expressions that are not good
  • Always touching my nose
  • I have a habit of inhaling strongly through my nose (due to nasal congestion). It makes me feel better, but I know it's unusual.
